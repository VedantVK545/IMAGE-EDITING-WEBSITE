HOW TO USE

Open your browser and navigate to the home page of your website.

On the home page, you should see an option to upload an image. Click on the "Choose File" button to select an image from your computer. Ensure that the image file is in one of the allowed formats: PNG, WEBP, JPG, or JPEG.

After selecting an image, you will have options to choose the type of operation you want to perform on the image. The available operations might include converting to grayscale, converting to webp, converting to jpg, or converting to png. Select the desired operation from the dropdown menu or buttons provided.

Once you have selected the image and the operation, click on the "Submit" button to start the image processing.

After submitting, the website will process the image according to the selected operation. This might take a few moments. Once the processing is complete, a message will appear with a link to view the processed image. Click on the provided link to view or download your edited image.
